3DEXPERIENCE. CAD and PLM.

The 3DEXPERIENCE platform © by Dassault Systèmes © promises to harmonize the products of the future with nature and life. This platform offers powerful Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) tools. These tools allow 3D modeling of products, such as cars, airplanes, shampoo bottles, and integrated circuits. The 3DEXPERIENCE also enables physically realistic simulation of product behavior and the design of industrial processes for production, machining, assembly, packaging, and product delivery.

This tremendous power comes with complexity. Adopting the 3DEXPERIENCE in your design processes is a strategic choice that requires careful consideration and meticulous planning. A successful 3DEXPERIENCE integration project can propel your company ahead of the competition in a sustainable way.

Embedded Systems. Internet of Things.

Many objects around us contain integrated circuits and are controlled by software: coffee makers, washing machines, drones, smartwatches, vacuum cleaners, thermostats, medical devices, and more.

The Internet of Things (IoT) promises that more and more objects will be interconnected and smarter.

These objects are autonomous, specialized, and combine electronic components (hardware) and software components (firmware). They are complex mechatronic systems that require multi-disciplinary engineering: mechanical, electronic, and software. Such objects are referred to as embedded systems.

Developing an embedded system is a complex and ambitious project that requires collaboration among engineers from different disciplines, each with different backgrounds, experiences, vocabularies, expectations, and constraints.

In addition to mechanical and electronic challenges, embedded systems pose significant software engineering challenges: real-time response at fixed frequencies (low latency), power consumption, minimalist and customized operating systems, system administration, cybersecurity, parallelism, concurrency, and more.

With over 15 years of experience in C++ and contribution to the development of projects in robotics, motion control, and the medical field, Loïc Yvonnet can assist you in overcoming the challenges that your embedded/IoT project may face.

Training. Always up-to-date.

Software engineering is constantly evolving. New technologies and trends emerge regularly. In this industry, it's not possible to build a career solely on the knowledge gained at college. Continuous learning is essential.

There are numerous ways to stay up-to-date: specialized journals and blogs, technical books, podcasts, meetups, conferences, social networks, communities, and more. Every professional should establish a strategy to stay current in their field.

Sometimes, the most effective way to quickly advance on a subject is to attend dedicated training. This approach offers the advantage of getting immediate answers to your questions and addressing your specific needs.
